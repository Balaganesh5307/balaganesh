ABOUT ART

Drawing is a form of art that involves marking a two-dimensional material like paper with lines, marks, and areas of tone. Artists use various tools, such as pencils, pastels, charcoal, and ink, to create drawings. A quick, freehand drawing, usually not intended as a finished work, is sometimes called a sketch.
